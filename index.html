Creamy Pasta Casserole With Tomatoes and Mozzarella

A pasta casserole is the perfect family meal if you want it to be quick and easy. Of course, you can use any kind of pasta, but you can also add other vegetables to the casserole. I grate the vegetables because my kids don't eat vegetables and I can hide them so well. If I'm in the mood for fish or meat, I add 2 cans of tuna or chicken if I had some leftover from the roast chicken the day before. Pasta casserole gratinated with cheese tastes good to almost all children. It doesn't always have to be spaghetti with tomato sauce on the table.
